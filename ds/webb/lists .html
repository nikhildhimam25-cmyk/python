How to Bake a Cake

  1. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 180°C (350°F) and preheat for 10 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Baking Pan: Grease with butter and dust with flour or use baking paper.
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix gently.
  4. Beat Butter & Sugar: Mix until light and fluffy.
  5. Add Eggs & Vanilla: Add eggs one by one, mix well, and add vanilla essence.
